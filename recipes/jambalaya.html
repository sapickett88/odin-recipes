Jambalaya

Description:

A spicy, all-in-one meal, that is as easy as throwing all the ingredients into a slow cooker, and spooning it into a bowl later that evening.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 4 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 2 large cans diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 3-4 cups chicken, cubed
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4-1/3 cup your favorite hot sauce
  • 2 lbs frozen shrimp
  • 12 oz Andouille sausage
  • 1/8-1/4 cup Cajun seasoning, see below
  • 2 cups rice, cooked
  • 3 cups okra
Recipe to make your own Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tbsp coarse black pepper
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 2 1/2 tbsp sea salt
  • 1 tbsp cayenne pepper

Steps:

  1. Turn your slow cooker on HIGH while you get your ingredients ready
  2. Spray or apply coconut oil to the insert of your slow cooker
  3. Place bell peppers, onions, celery, garlic, and bay leaves into the slow cooker
  4. Pour the diced tomatoes over the vegetables
  5. Add the chicken cubes, chicken broth, and hot sauce
  6. Cover with lid, turn the slow cooker down to LOW , and cook for 6 hours
  7. At 30 minutes before serving, add the shrimp, sausage, okra, and seasonings
  8. Allow them to cook until the shrimp is pink and the okra is cooked
  9. Serve over rice
