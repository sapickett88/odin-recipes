Pulled Pork and Sweet Potato Hash

Description:

Delicious breakfast that can be egg-free if you wish . Also great as a post-workout meal.

Ingredients:

For the Pulled Pork
  • 2 lbs boneless pork, shoulder roast or Boston butt roast
  • 1/2 tbsp sea salt
For the Hash
  • 1 1/2 lbs sweet potatoes, peeled and grated
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1/4 + 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 6 eggs, optional

Steps:

  1. The day before you plan to eat this, start the pulled pork.
  2. Place the roast in the slow cooker, and rub it all over with the 1/2 tbsp sea salt.
  3. Cover and turn the heat on LOW for 12 to 14 hours.
  4. When it's done, move the meat to a large bowl and shred.
  5. Also done the day before, peel and grate the sweet potato, it should dry out a little bit over night, and work better for the hash.
  6. Refrigerate until the next morning
  7. Heat a very large skillet over medium-high heat and melt 1 tbsp ghee in the pan.
  8. Cook and stir the onions with 1/4 tsp salt until softened and slightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  9. Add the sweet potatoes, then add another tbsp ghee and another1/2 tsp more salt.
  10. Allow the bottom of the sweet potatoes to brown over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes.
  11. Then flip sections with a spatula and let the other side brown, another 3 to 4 minutes.
  12. Turn off heat and mix in the pulled pork
  13. OPTIONAL Form wells in the hash with a spoon and crack the eggs into them. Cover the pan so the eggs steam through.
