Chicken Parmesan

Description:

This is a variation of one of the first meals I cooked. Whenever I eat it, it brings back fond memories of being in the kitchen with my step mother. It's an easy to make, delicious, Italian classic!

Ingredients:

Marinara Sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 14 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp sugar
Chicken
  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp plain (all purpose) flour
  • 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3/4 cup finely grates parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 ball of mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
To Serve
  • cooked spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp sliced fresh basil

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F and put a large baking dish to one side
  2. Start with the marinara sauce. Heat the olive oil in a medium- sized saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5-6 minutes, stirring until softened.
  3. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant
  4. Add the tomato sauce, oregano, and sugar. Stir and bring to a boil, then simmer gently for 10 minutes. Turn off heat.
  5. Meanwhile prepare the chicken. Place the chicken breasts flat on a board and slice in half through the middle, so you have 4 equal sizes. Dust on both sides with the flour.
  6. Add the eggs to a bowl and give them a quick whisk.
  7. Add the panko, paprika, salt, pepper, half the parmesan, and the olive oil to a second bowl. Stir until Panko is evenly coated
  8. Coat a chicken fillet in the egg, and then dip in panko until completely covered. Then place in the large baking dish. Repeat This for the remaining 3 fillets
  9. Place in the oven for 10 minutes until the breadcrumbs are lightly browned.
  10. Top the chicken fillets with the remaining marinara sauce. Arrange the slices of mozzarella on top of the chicken and sprinkle on the remaining parmesan and dried oregano.
  11. Place back in the oven for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is golden and piping hot throughout
  12. Serve the chicken with cooked spaghetti and sprinkle on fresh basil before serving
